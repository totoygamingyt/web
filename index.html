About Me

Hello there, I'm TotoyGamingYT, a Filipino content creator and YouTube streamer. I'm well-known for my daily or weekly updates on what's going on in Pet Simulator X!

Before Roblox (2020-2021)

Before I started making Roblox content, I used to create Minecraft videos on my YouTube channel. My Minecraft videos mainly focused on mining blocks. I also began to upload videos about Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Call of Duty: Mobile. Unfortunately, I had to delete almost all of the content related to Minecraft, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, and Call of Duty: Mobile from my channel due to several copyright issues. (I had no knowledge or understanding about copyright at that time)

Roblox (2021-present)

I started on Roblox by creating an informative and update channel for the popular game "Pet Simulator X". I made videos about leaks, gameplay, and skits related to Pet Simulator X and more. My YouTube channel started to gain traction in October 2021, and I continued to upload daily until November 16, 2021, when I reached my first 1,000 subscribers.
