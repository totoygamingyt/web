About Me

Hello there, I'm TotoyGamingYT, a Filipino content creator and YouTube streamer. I'm well-known for my daily or weekly updates on what's going on in Pet Simulator X!

Before Roblox (2020-2021)

TotoyGamingYT was a Minecraft YouTuber before he entered Roblox. All of his Minecraft content was focused on mining blocks. He also started to upload videos of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Call of Duty: Mobile. However, due to several copyright issues, he had to delete almost all of the content related to Minecraft, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, and Call of Duty: Mobile from his channel. (I had no knowledge or understanding about copyright at that time)

Roblox (2021-present)

He started on Roblox as an Informative or Update Channel for the Roblox Game "Pet Simulator X", Doing Leaks Videos, Pet Simulator X Videos, Pet Simulator X Skits and more. He started growing on YouTube in October 2021, He continued to upload daily until November 16, 2021, when he reached his First 1,000 subscribers.
